Renewable energy sources are increasingly seen as vital to combating climate change. Solar panels, wind turbines, and hydroelectric dams are among the most common technologies harnessing natural energy. These methods generate electricity without emitting greenhouse gases, making them more sustainable than traditional fossil fuels. Governments and private companies are investing heavily in renewable energy infrastructure, recognizing its potential to reduce carbon footprints and secure energy independence. As technology advances, the efficiency and affordability of renewable energy solutions continue to improve, making them accessible to more people worldwide.
